About Me

My name is Osvaldo Armas, but I often go by Ozzy. I am your classic, sassy, latino, nerd. I love tinkering and making things I can bring to life; where people and technology shake hands. Some of my goals are to make a positive difference, become a college professor, and bring the power of Cuban Coffee to the American North.

When my parents and I migrated to the United States (Northern Cuba/Miami) we were very fortunate (sarcasm) to arrive during the recent recession. As a result, my dad and I found ourselves performing a myriad of jobs. We dug holes on concrete to locate pipelines, and delivered newspaper and pizza. My dad later became an Uber driver and I was able to continue focusing on my education. During my senior year, at a new high school, I was fortunate enough to find a group of driven individuals. Together, we found S(A^3)S Robotics (FRC TEAM 5329).I was the president and team captain.

Now, I am a proud, fifth(?)-year, Yellow Jacket, at Georgia Tech. I'm a Computer Science major concentrating in devices and intelligence. I wish to pursue a Ph.D. in Human Robot Interaction. Once I have gathered plenty of experience from internships and academia, I plan to start my own company where I'll strive to see my goal of a robot in every home across the globe.
